URL to Brit & Co

Trigger

The trigger is hover that intitates the mocro interaction for both the read more and for the photo/text box expansion.

Rules & Feedback

Hover over Medium Micro-Interaction

Photo & Text Box

The photo is centered, the white text box with text is centered in the middle, and is slightly towards the bottom in order to show more of the image. When hovered over, the Picture moves to the right and sizes down while the text box moves to the left. Then, the Text box moves from the center of the image to expanding, and revealing more text as it moves to the left.

Outline

There is a purple outline surrounds the image. The outline is not outside of the image box, but inside, giving it an inner border. The outline is then outlining the Image and Text Box.

Hover Over Read More

Photo

The "Read More" button is completely yellow. The button is a square that surround the entire text with the text centered.

  • When you hover over the Read More, the Yellow box becomes smaller, and becomes an underline under the text to signify that it is an interactable/clickable link.

Feel of the Microinteraction

wanted to have a flowy, and gentle interaction, nothing that was too sharp that presented an anxious emotion

  • The website promotes self-care, so I wanted to eminate that through the micro-interaction itself, showing the gradual change

Feedback

The user sees all of the different rules except for the backend.

  • The photo and text box expanding and collapsing. When the mouse remains hovered over the medium micro-interaction, the user can then hover over the "read more." When hovered over the "Read More" the small Micro-interacion then occurs.

Loops & Modes

These are only Modes, nothing is looping. When the micro interaction is triggered, there is aditional information about the article that is seen. (i.e. "Let's talk about your brain."). When the mouse is hovered over the "Read more" within the Micro-interaction, this is neither a mode or a loop.
